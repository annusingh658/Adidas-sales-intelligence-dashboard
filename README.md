# 📊 Adidas Sales Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview

The Adidas Sales Intelligence Dashboard is an interactive business analytics application developed using Python, Streamlit, Pandas, and Plotly.

The dashboard transforms raw Adidas sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and performance analysis. It enables users to explore sales trends, retailer performance, regional growth, and overall business performance in a simple and intuitive way.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Retailer Performance Analysis

Analyze and compare sales generated by different retailers using interactive bar charts.

### Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

Track sales performance over time and identify growth patterns using time-series visualizations.

### State-wise Performance Dashboard

Compare total sales and units sold across different states through dual-axis analytics.

### Regional Sales Intelligence

Explore sales contribution across regions and cities using hierarchical treemap visualization.

### Interactive Data Exploration

View and explore processed business data directly from the dashboard.

### Report Download Functionality

Export analytical reports and datasets for further business analysis.
